## About Salman
Salman Khan studies epistemology with a focus on economics and entrepreneurship. His practice centers on allocating capital, examining how knowledge is formed and tested in markets, and documenting the process. Thumbliner is his online persona.

## About this site
This site is public-facing documentation and a living knowledge base. It does not follow a rigid hierarchical structure; instead, it reflects a network of connected ideas, encouraging exploration through links and search. Built with Jekyll and the Just the Docs theme—both chosen for philosophical alignment.
