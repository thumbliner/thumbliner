# Salman Khan

TLDR: Salman is a business-obsessed generalist who can read, write, do basic math, and persist until things get done; his Afghani ancestors moved to Hyderabad ~200 years ago to trade, and he grew up in Jeddah. He’s worked across companies of many sizes and stages, jumping between marketing, analytics, finance, investor relations, and whatever else was needed, while studying and even teaching business. Since finishing formal school, he’s gone deeper into economics, capital allocation, and management science; he reads 10-Ks for fun and vacations in Sarnia. Do you really want to know more?
