## Author
Salman Khan studies epistemology at the intersection of economics and entrepreneurship. The backend runs as a research pipeline with concept-indexed, atomic notes, experiments and market observations, and iterative memos that probe how knowledge forms under pressure. The frontend presents distilled write-ups and working notes under the Thumbliner persona.

Salman Khan is an applied researcher in economics and epistemology, focused on how entrepreneurs create and transmit knowledge in markets. He runs a capital-allocation practice built on a concept-indexed pipeline of atomic notes; select outputs are shared under the Thumbliner persona.

## Website
This site works as both interface and engine. On the surface stands a fast public knowledge base built with Jekyll and the Just the Docs theme, tuned for search, interlinking, and reading. Under the hood it compiles from a non-hierarchical network of evergreen notes, and as the backend graph grows and connects, the frontend regenerates to surface new pathways and syntheses, bringing the research work and the published layer together in one continuous system.
