## About Salman
Salman Khan studies epistemology with a focus on economics and entrepreneurship. The practice centers on allocating capital, examining how knowledge is formed and tested in markets, and documenting the process so decisions and assumptions remain legible over time.

## About this site
This site is public-facing documentation and a living knowledge base. It does not follow a rigid hierarchical structure; instead, it reflects a network of connected ideas, encouraging exploration through links and search. Built with Jekyll and the Just the Docs theme—both chosen for simplicity and modularity—and refined with a few custom design elements.
