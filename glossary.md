# Glossary 

**Definitions:** Explicit meaning-fixes for key terms that determine their reference and application conditions in the discussion.

**Common Ground:** The propositions mutually taken as accepted and available as shared premises in the conversation.

**Deductions:** Valid inferences in which the conclusion follows necessarily from the premises under the stipulated meanings and rules of inference.

**Conclusions:** The proposition an argument aims to establish as supported by its premises and evidence.

**Assumptions:** Unproven premises adopted for the purposes of an argument or inquiry.
