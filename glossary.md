# Glossary 

---

**Definition:** Explicit meaning-fixes for key terms that determine their reference and application conditions in the discussion.

**Common Ground:** The propositions mutually taken as accepted and available as shared premises in the conversation.

**Deduction:** Valid inference in which the conclusion follows necessarily from the premises under the stipulated meanings and rules of inference.

**Conclusion:** The proposition an argument aims to establish as supported by its premises and evidence.

**Assumption:** Unproven premise adopted for the purposes of an argument or inquiry.
