# Josep “Pep” Guardiola Sala
_Manager, Manchester City Football Club_

born 18 January 1971
Santpedor, Catalonia

One of football’s defining managers: a meticulous philosopher and organiser whose teams are built on simple ideas, attention to detail, and consistency.
