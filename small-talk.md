Salaam

How do you do?  
I’m fine. And you?<br>

Can you believe this weather?  
I find this city most beautiful in the rain.<br>

What are you up to?  
Studying music and metaphor.<br>

What about `insert trend`?  
I don’t know. What about it?<br>

Huh…<br>

So, who are you, really?  
I am your fellow human.<br>

Any flaws?  
The standard.<br>

Aren’t you outraged by `insert cue`?  
Generally yes, but not particularly.<br>

Then you should read more about it.  
Alright, will add to the list.<br>

Peace
